InteractiveMidiImprovisor uses a Beat Similarity Matrix (BSM) to detect the time signature of a song. BSMs algo generate some interesting visualizations of the structure of a song:
  • Bach Minuet (1)
  • Bach Minuet (2)
  • Battle Hymn of the Republic
  • Bring Back My Bonnie to Me
  • Somewhere Over the Rainbow
  • This Train (is bound for glory)
  • Amazing Grace
  • Ode to Joy
